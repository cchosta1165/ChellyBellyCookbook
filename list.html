Baked Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 4oz can of green chiles
  • 4oz can of adobe chipotle peppers
  • 1 TBL garlic powder
  • 1 TBL onion powder
  • 1.5 lb of boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 - 14.5 oz can of refried beans
  • 4oz of shredded mexican cheese
  • 10 stand up taco shells
  • 6oz of salsa ( I used medium)
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • Toppings: sour cream , avocado, red onion, and more CHEESE

Instructions

  • Marinade
  1. Combine the chicken, green chiles, adobe peppers, garlic and onion powder into a gallon size bag.
  2. Marinade in the fridge for a minimum of 15 minutes
  3. Pour the marinaded chicken into the instapot
  4. Cook on manual for 25 minutes
    • Bake chicken at 375oF for 30 mintes, if you don't own an instapot
  5. Once chicken is cooked, use forks to shred the chicken, set aside
  • Remaining Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 375oF
  2. Spread refriend beans at the bottom of each taco shell
  3. Top each shell with shredded chicken
  4. Sprinkle some salsa and top with shredded cheese
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes, until cheese has melted
  6. Top your taco with your favorite toppings!

Enjoy!

Crispy Onion Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 lb of boneless chicken breasts, cut length-wise
  • 3-4 large eggs
  • onion powder
  • 1 cup of all-purpse flour
  • 1 container of crispy fried onions
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF
  2. Grab 3 shallow dishes
  3. Place the flour, eggs (whisked), and fried onions into each of the dishes
  4. Lightly season each chicken breast with salt, pepper, and onion powder
  5. One by one, coat each chicken breast with flour, then into the eggs mixture, then into the fried onions.
  6. Place chicken on a baking sheet
  7. Bake chicken for 30 minutes, until golden brown and crispy
    • If using airfyer, fry at 375oF for 20 minutes. Flip each breast after 10 minutes

Enjoy!

Nannie's Chicken and Dumplings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 tbl of butter, unsalted
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 qt of chicken broth
  • 3 cups of cooked chicken

Instructions

  1. In a bowl combine the flour, baking powder, and salt
  2. Cut the butter into the dry ingredients with a fork
  3. Stir in milk and form dough into a ball
  4. Heavily flour a surface for rolling out the dough and flour the rolling pin
  5. Roll out the dough to about 2/3 the size of a cookie sheet, keeping it heavily floured
  6. Bring broth to a boil
  7. While the broth is at a slow boil, tear pieces from the rolled-out dough, approx 1 inch square
  8. Keep coating the pieces with flour and drop into slow boiling broth
  9. The extra flour will thicken up the broth
  10. Add the chicken and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, covered

Enjoy!

Chili

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey, I used turkey
  • 1 - 14.5oz can of pinto or kidney beans
  • 1 can of mushrooms
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cans of rotel
  • 2 - 32oz cans of whole peeled tomatoes
  • 4 - 1oz packets of Bloomers chili powder
  • 2 cartons of Bloomers chili base, 1 mild and 1 hot
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook ground turkey (or beef) with onions. Drain excess fat
  2. Add Bloomers base and simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Add all cans to a dutch oven pot
  4. Add chili packets and the ground turkey with onions
  5. Bring to a boil and stir often
  6. Let it simmer on low for 2 hours
  7. Add your favorite toppings!

Enjoy!

Spicy Chicken Pesto Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbl minced garlic
  • 1/4 yellow onlin, chopped finely
  • 2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 3/4 cup basil pesto, I used store-bought
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 lb pasta of your choice, I used ziti
  • 3 tbl salted butter
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup basil, roughly chopped
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet with the olive oil and add onions and garlic
  2. Cook on a medium-low heat, until onions are translucent
  3. Reduce the heat to low, and add the tomato paste and red pepper flakes
  4. Cook for 3 minutes and pour the vodka in.
  5. Let it cook off for about 2 minutes until the alcohol has evaporated
  6. Stir in the milk and pesto and let it simmer for a few minutes
  7. Meanwhile salt a large stock pot of water and bring to a boil
  8. Cook the pasta to your liking and reserve about 1-1/2 cups of pasta water
  9. Add the pasta water to the pesto sauce, little by little, until you get a creamy texture
  10. Finish with the butter, parmesan, and basil
  11. Toss the pasta and shredded chicken into the sauce
  12. Top with extra Parmesan and basil

Enjoy!
